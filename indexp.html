About Us

CARE Stands for the Compendium of Rural Africa Enterprises. It is a continental vision birthed by Dr. Strive Masiyiwa with the focus of digitalization of the African Rural Enterprises in order for the World to know what innovative businesses are taking place in the Africa continent. This project can be likened to be the Google of Africa businesses. It is a platform where every form of data that relates to the africa business shall be found. Hence, CARE is the data bank of Africa businesses especially its Rural Enterprises.
